How insured commitments work

Four steps. No lock-in. No underutilization risk.

Archera sits between your cloud usage and your commitments, absorbing the risk that used to fall on your team.

01

Find spend you could commit to

Archera analyzes your cloud usage to identify where commitments would save money — if underutilization risk didn’t hold you back.

02

Choose RIs, SPs & CUDs with risk protection

Archera recommends plans to choose from — each with downside risk protection built in. Pay a monthly premium, we cover anything you don’t use.

03

Commitments live in your cloud org

Once you select a plan, Archera executes the commitment directly inside your organization. You keep full ownership and control of your billing.

04

If usage changes, you don’t pay for it

Archera covers unused commitments via reimbursements or by releasing you from the remaining term. You save. We absorb the variance.
